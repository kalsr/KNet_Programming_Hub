About These Applications

The KNet Application Hub brings together a suite of innovative, AI-powered tools designed to solve real-world challenges. From analyzing supply chain risks and monitoring cyber threats to managing dental practices and generating synthetic datasets, each program leverages the MCP (Model Control Pipeline) and advanced AI/ML methods to deliver insights, automation, and decision support. This hub is a one-stop gateway for exploring practical AI-driven applications across security, healthcare, and enterprise domains.

Agentic-AI Use_Case for Cyber-Threat_Detector

The Cyber-Threat-Detector harnesses Agentic AI to automatically monitor networks, endpoints, and cloud systems for emerging threats. Intelligent agents work together to spot anomalies, adapt detection in real time, and prioritize risks by severity. This smart, autonomous approach delivers actionable insights, reduces false positives, and helps security teams stay ahead of sophisticated cyberattacks.

Supply Chain Risk Estimator

The Supply Chain Risk Estimator analyzes SBOMs (Software Bills of Materials) to detect vulnerabilities, license risks, and supplier trust issues. Using an MCP (Model Control Pipeline) and AI/ML models, it classifies risks into Low, Medium, or High and visualizes them with charts and dashboards. Users can upload SBOM data, generate synthetic records, or export results for reporting.
