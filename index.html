The Supply Chain Risk Estimator analyzes SBOMs (Software Bills of Materials) to detect vulnerabilities, license risks, and supplier trust issues. Using an MCP (Model Control Pipeline) and AI/ML models, it classifies risks into Low, Medium, or High and visualizes them with charts and dashboards. Users can upload SBOM data, generate synthetic records, or export results for reporting.
